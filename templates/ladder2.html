The rook with the blue area acts as a barrier, disallowing the king to move left. We want to continue limiting where the king can move, so we move the rook on e4 to the next column to check the king.
With one rook blocking off the f file, and the other checking the kind on the g file, the king can only move to to h file.
We then move the rook that's farther away to check the king and control the only available file for the king, in this case the h file. Notice the rooks move, as if climbing a ladder, to chase the king.
