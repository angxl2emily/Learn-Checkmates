Queen + Helper Checkmate

{% endblock %} {% block screencaps %}
With only a queen cornering the king, the king can simply capture the queen to get out of check
With a helper, this case the bishop, protecting the queen, the king can no longer captures the queen, since that would put the king in check by the helper piece.
We want to be careful, however, that the queen is in the correct position to block off every one of the king's escape squares
